Ergo Proxy

"Ergo Proxy" is a complex, controversial, and ambiguous series. You could call it "neo-cyberpunk," or "a psychological road movie," or "a futuristic parable with a philosophical twist and gunplay"... It's a lot of things, too much in some places.

Beyond the plot and characters, "Ergo Proxy" contains a fair number of allusions, quotes, and references to cultural, philosophical, and other phenomena. Each viewer decides for themselves: simply watch without paying attention to the subtext, limit themselves to their existing knowledge, or dive into an abyss of unfamiliar information.

This site is intended to fill in the blanks and provide a basis for reflection for those who cannot help but think.
